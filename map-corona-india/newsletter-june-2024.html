Energy
CERC issues Renewable Energy Tariff Regulations, 2024
 
The Renewable Energy Tariff Regulations, 2024 issued by the Central Electricity Regulatory Commission (CERC) of India are effective from July 1, 2024, and will remain in force until March 31, 2027, unless revised earlier. These define various terms such as biomass, biogas, capital cost, and renewable energy sources. Continue Reading

DERC releases draft regulation for green power obligations on discoms
 
The Delhi Electricity Regulatory Commission (DERC) has released its draft regulations for renewable purchase obligation (RPO), mandating a 29.91 per cent green energy purchase this year for all stakeholders, including discoms. RPOs mandate all electricity distribution licensees to purchase energy from renewable sources. Continue Reading
