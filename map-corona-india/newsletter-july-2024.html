Electric Vehicles
Electric two and three-wheeler subsidies extended till September 2024
 
The Electric Mobility Promotion Scheme (EMPS) for two- and three-wheelers has been extended by the Ministry of Heavy Industries (MHI) until September 30, 2024. For electric two-wheelers and electric three-wheelers, the initiative offers a subsidy of up to ₹ 10,000 and ₹ 50,000, respectively. Continue Reading

Environment ministry brings draft to adjudicate pollution offences
 
The Environment Ministry has released the draft Environment (Protection) (Amendment) Rules, 2024, which establish a new system for deciding environmental offenses under the Air and Water Act and the Environment Protection Act. These rules give bureaucrats quasi-judicial authority to investigate and punish environmental offenses. Continue Reading
