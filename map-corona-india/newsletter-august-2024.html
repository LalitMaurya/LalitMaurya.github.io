Western Ghats ESA

The Central government released the sixth draft notification, designating 56,825.7 square kilometers of the Western Ghats as an Ecologically Sensitive Area (ESA) spanning across Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu. Continue Reading

New Construction and Demolition Waste Management Rules to take effect
 
The Ministry of Environment, Forests, and Climate Change issued the Construction and Demolition Waste Management Rules, 2024, set to take effect on April 1, 2025. Energy
Bill in Parliament aims to ensure policy stability for oil and gas sector
 
The Oilfields (Regulation and Development) Amendment Bill, 2024, introduced in the Rajya Sabha, seeks to provide oil and gas producers with policy stability, options for international arbitration in disputes, and the potential for longer leases. This bill is designed to enhance investor confidence by addressing numerous long-standing concerns. Continue Reading
