End-of-life Vehicles
Delhi government issues guidelines for handling end-of-life vehicles in public places
 
The Delhi government has issued guidelines for managing end-of-life vehicles in public areas. The owners of impounded vehicles must pay a penalty of Rs 10,000 for four-wheelers and Rs 5,000 for two-wheelers before their vehicles can be released. Continue Reading

Health
ICMR released 17 dietary guidelines
 
Estimates show that 56.4% of the total disease burden in India is due to unhealthy diets, the ICMR said on May 8, as it released 17 dietary guidelines to meet the requirements of essential nutrients and prevent non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as obesity and diabetes. Continue Reading

Gujarat commission waived technical feasibility requirement for rooftop solar systems under 10 kW
 
The Draft Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (Net Metering Rooftop Solar PV Grid Interactive Systems) (Fourth Amendment) Regulations, 2024, introduces specific requirements for low- and high-tension customers installing rooftop solar systems. This is aimed towards improving the integration of higher solar capacities into the grid while maintaining fair cost distribution. Continue Reading
